My Neighborhood, My Life: Neighborhood Wellbeing Survey


Goal

To better understand how to better improve neighborhood Wellbeing, Health, Environment and Equity.

Where:

Twin Cities Metro Area

How to participate:

Distribute the neighborhood survey about infrastructure, leisure and household amenities. The survey is to be completed by one adult in each household.
Survey Link

Description:

Survey data from many different households across the Twin Cities are needed to better understand the types of household and neighborhood infrastructures that are most important for residents overall wellbeing. By better understanding residents opinions, the survey seeks to create sustainable, healthy communities. Participants can view the overall results of the survey.
