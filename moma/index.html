What we offer

We are a community of music learners and lovers at all skill levels. Our collaborative virtual learning program offers high quality music instruction in the areas of musical performance, composition, theory, and history. Our community provides a supportive digital space to share music, practice and perform together for our community

Music Instruction

We offer live online music instruction. Our teachers are both performing and recording artists as well as professional educators; that's our secret sauce!

Learning Resources

Self-paced videos and lessons to get you playing fast, on your own schedule. Accredited teacher created curriculum helps foster a deeper appreciation of music and meets Minnesota's performing arts standards.

Jam Spaces & Concert Hall

We use cutting edge digital tools to rehearse, find practice partners, and host live events.

For the love of playing music!

There is really nothing like the feeling that playing music can bring to you and the people around you. The Mock Online Music Academy (MOMA) is dedicated to helping you experience this through high quality instruction from our teachers and community members. Our learning environment is used by learners of all ages, skill levels and abilities.

  • Step 1

    We want you to enjoy playing music for a lifetime

    We have created a brief survey to help us recommend a learning path and teacher for your needs and goals.

  • Step 2

    Prepare for Boarding

    You are in control! Choose your learning path and teacher. Then receive training on our easy to use technology and join our community

  • Step Three

    Begin your Journey

    Whether you've chosen our proven fourteen week program or customize your own path, our instructors will guide you towards your music goals. While mastering your instrument, you will also learn about the musical traditions and musicians who influenced the music we play today with through digital storytelling and listening. By understanding their journey and music you will see yourself in the story and become a better musician>

  • July 2014

    Playing together

    Playing together You have mastered the basics of music and can now experience the thrill of making music together with others. Call it a band, ensamble, free jam or orchestra, find other community musicians to play together with. Watch recitals and ensembles by your fellow students and teachers. When you're ready, share your music with our supportive community
