Food Systems

Local food production has important ecological, economic and social benefits for residents.

Research also suggest it improves our lives and wellbeing. Community gardens help build community by creating a place to meet others and get to know their neighbors. Gardens also provide a sense of ownership, connection, and responsibility.



"The social connections that gardens foster may be an equally (or more) important policy rationale for supporting gardens as part of neighborhood green space. Protections against the sale and redevelopment of garden sites may need to be provided directly by the city or in partnership with non-profit and/or quasi-public entities. "

Adapted or direct qoute from Sustainable Healthy Cities Journal Brief - 2019, No. 10 - Community Gardens and Social Benefits

Food & Health

Nourishing ourselves and the world

Access to a variety of fresh and healthy ingredients and food options is important in staying healthy. A healthy diet is improves heart, bones, brain, mood and memory function. Poor diets are linked to diseases such as diabetes and obesity.

Becasue of soild depletion "Fruits and vegetables grown decades ago were much richer in vitamins and minerals than the varieties most of us get today."

Journal of the American College of Nutrition, www.jacn.org; Kushi Institute, www.kushiinstitute.org; Organic Consumers Association, www.organicconsumers.org.

Percent of people under nourished by world region

environment

50% of all crops grown are from four plants: Soy, Corn, Wheat, and Rice

Even with an increased knowledge in sustainable farming techniques, large scale industrial farming is a primary source of water and air pollution, habitat loss and deforestation. Crop and bio-diversity has declined steadily since the 1980's.

Our best estimates are that global food demand will increase between 60 and 100 percent by mid-century. Even if we meet this demand through sustainable intensification of agriculture, nearly all remaining forests and grasslands in the tropics that are suitable for agriculture would need to be plowed up.
